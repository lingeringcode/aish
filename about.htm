FAQ

What is this?

"As It So Happens" is my little pet site, and a forked project from Lockridge's Rhetorlist, which tracks books across the discipline. While Lockridge is tracking books, I am merely offerring a single place to see articles with standardized RSS feeds, as they so happen to be published.

Why isn't [insert x and y or z journal] included?

Any journal not listed here is by no means a product of my position about the journal. This is a new project, so I'm open to suggestions. I am sure new distinctly organized pages can eventually be created to best categorize journals. If you think a journal should be included, and it has an RSS feed with standardized XML format, please feel free to message me about it.

How did you make this?

The source is on Github. Feel free to contribute with a pull request or fork and make your own.

Who maintains this?

Hey, hey. I'm Chris Lindgren. You can email me (lindgren@vt.edu) or tweet me (@lndgrn) with your suggestions or concerns.
